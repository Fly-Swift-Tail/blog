## What You Need to Know Before Booking

### Understanding Empty Leg Flights

Empty leg flights occur when a private jet must reposition for its next charter. For example, if a jet flies from New York to Miami for a client, it might need to return empty to New York for the next booking. Rather than absorb the full cost of an empty flight, operators offer these repositioning flights at significant discounts.

### Key Limitations to Consider

- **One-way only**: Empty legs are typically point-to-point flights
- **Fixed schedule**: Departure times are set by the original charter
- **Limited flexibility**: Route and timing changes are usually not possible
- **Quick booking required**: Deals can disappear within hours

## Step 3: Evaluate Empty Leg Options

### Aircraft Assessment

**Size and Capacity**:

- **Light Jets**: 4-6 passengers, 1-3 hour flights (Citation CJ series, Phenom 300)
- **Midsize Jets**: 6-8 passengers, 3-5 hour flights (Citation XLS, Hawker 800XP)
- **Heavy Jets**: 8-14 passengers, transcontinental (Falcon 900, Gulfstream G450)

**Key Questions to Ask**:

1. What's included in the quoted price?
2. Are there additional fees (catering, ground transportation, fuel surcharges)?
3. What's the cancellation policy?
4. Is the aircraft substitutable if there are mechanical issues?

### Pricing Evaluation

**Typical Empty Leg Pricing**:

- **Light Jets**: $1,200-3,500 per hour (vs $2,500-6,000 regular)
- **Midsize Jets**: $2,500-5,500 per hour (vs $4,000-8,500 regular)
- **Heavy Jets**: $4,000-8,500 per hour (vs $6,500-15,000 regular)

**Hidden Costs to Watch For**:

- Federal Excise Tax (7.5% in the US)
- Segment fees ($4.30 per passenger per segment)
- International fees (customs, handling)
- Overnight crew costs (for repositioning flights)
- Catering and ground transportation

## Step 4: Master the Booking Process

### Act Quickly but Carefully

**When You Find a Good Option**:

1. **Verify availability immediately** - Call within 15 minutes of seeing the listing
2. **Ask about the original charter** - Understand why the empty leg exists
3. **Request aircraft tail number** - Verify the specific aircraft you'll be flying
4. **Confirm all inclusions** - Ensure catering, ground transport, etc. are clarified

### Booking Timeline

**Day of Discovery**:

- Contact operator within 15 minutes of finding the flight
- Request detailed quote with all fees included
- Ask about cancellation and change policies

**Same Day or Next Day**:

- Provide passenger manifest and travel documents
- Arrange payment (credit card, wire transfer, or account credit)
- Confirm departure logistics and ground transportation

### Payment and Contracts

**Typical Payment Terms**:

- **Deposit**: 25-50% upon booking confirmation
- **Balance**: Due 24-48 hours before departure
- **Payment methods**: Credit card (3% fee), wire transfer, or account credit

**Contract Elements to Review**:

- Cancellation policy (both ways)
- Aircraft substitution rights
- Weather delay procedures
- Baggage limitations
- Passenger change policies

## Step 5: Prepare for Departure

### Documentation Requirements

**Domestic Flights**:

- Government-issued photo ID for all passengers
- Passenger manifest submitted 24 hours prior
- Any special dietary requirements or accessibility needs

**International Flights**:

- Valid passports for all passengers
- Visas (if required for destination)
- Customs pre-clearance information
- International passenger manifest

### Day of Travel

**Arrival Time**: Plan to arrive 15-30 minutes before departure
**Security**: Private terminals have minimal security compared to commercial
**Baggage**: Confirm weight and size limitations with operator
**Changes**: Last-minute passenger changes may incur fees

## Common Booking Mistakes to Avoid

### Timing Errors

- **Waiting too long to book** - Empty legs sell quickly
- **Not confirming 24 hours prior** - Flights can be cancelled
- **Ignoring weather backup plans** - Private jets are more weather-sensitive

### Financial Mistakes

- **Not reading the fine print** - Hidden fees can double your cost
- **Assuming all prices are comparable** - Compare total cost, not just hourly rates
- **Not understanding cancellation policies** - You may lose your deposit

### Logistical Oversights

- **Forgetting international documentation** - Can result in denied boarding
- **Not confirming passenger changes** - Additional fees may apply
- **Overlooking ground transportation** - Private airports may be remote
